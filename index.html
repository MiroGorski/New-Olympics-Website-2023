About Us

Our mission is to promote health, wellness, and team spirit among AUBG students, faculty, and staff. The AUBG Olympics is open to everyone and includes a wide range of events, from sports competitions to trivia contests and talent shows.

Events

  • Swimming
  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
  • Table tennis
  • Chess
  • Talent show
  • Trivia night
